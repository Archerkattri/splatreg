# splatreg — icon brief (for image generation)

A minimalist, modern logo for **splatreg**, a library that registers and merges two 3D Gaussian splats into one aligned frame (the inverse of gsplat). Show two soft, overlapping clouds of blurred "splat" dots snapping into alignment — two offset clusters converging into a single shape — to convey registration and merging. Use a cool geometric palette (teal/cyan for one cloud, a warm accent for the other) on a transparent background, in a flat vector style with soft circular dots, no skeuomorphism and no 3D. Keep it sharp at small sizes; it should read clearly as a precise geometry / 3D-vision tool.

Deliverables: a 512×512 transparent-PNG logo (square) and a 1280×640 PNG social-preview banner.
